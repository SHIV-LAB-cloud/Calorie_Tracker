Weight Gain Diet Plan

Day 1

  • Breakfast: Smoothie with whole milk, banana, peanut butter, and protein powder
  • Lunch: Chicken and avocado sandwich on whole grain bread
  • Dinner: Grilled steak with sweet potatoes and a side of mixed vegetables
  • Snacks: Greek yogurt with honey, handful of almonds

Day 2

  • Breakfast: Oatmeal with whole milk, topped with nuts and dried fruits
  • Lunch: Quinoa salad with chickpeas, avocado, and olive oil dressing
  • Dinner: Baked salmon with brown rice and steamed broccoli
  • Snacks: Cheese and whole grain crackers, protein bar

Day 3

  • Breakfast: Whole grain toast with avocado and scrambled eggs
  • Lunch: Tuna salad with mixed greens and a side of whole grain bread
  • Dinner: Spaghetti with meatballs and a side salad
  • Snacks: Smoothie with whole milk, berries, and protein powder

Tips for Healthy Weight Gain:

  • Eat More Frequently: Have 5-6 smaller meals throughout the day.
  • Choose Nutrient-Rich Foods: Focus on whole grains, lean proteins, healthy fats, and dairy.
  • Drink High-Calorie Smoothies: Use whole milk, nut butters, and protein powders.
  • Incorporate Strength Training: Exercise to build muscle mass.
  • Stay Consistent: Stick to your plan and adjust as needed.
