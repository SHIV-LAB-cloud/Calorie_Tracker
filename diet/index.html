Budget Gym Diet Plan

Breakfast

Oatmeal with Peanut Butter and Banana

  • 1 cup of oats
  • 1 tablespoon of peanut butter
  • 1 banana, sliced
  • Optional: a sprinkle of cinnamon

Mid-Morning Snack

Greek Yogurt with Honey and Nuts

  • 1 cup of plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon of honey
  • A handful of mixed nuts

Lunch

Chicken and Rice Bowl

  • 1 cup of cooked brown rice
  • 1 grilled chicken breast (seasoned with salt, pepper, and herbs)
  • Steamed vegetables (like broccoli, carrots, and peas)
  • Optional: a drizzle of olive oil

Afternoon Snack

Apple and Cottage Cheese

  • 1 apple, sliced
  • 1/2 cup of cottage cheese

Dinner

Lentil Soup with Whole Grain Bread

  • 1 cup of lentils
  • 1 diced onion, 2 diced carrots, and 2 diced celery stalks
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 4 cups of vegetable broth
  • Season with garlic, cumin, and bay leaves
  • Serve with a slice of whole grain bread

Evening Snack

Protein Shake

  • 1 scoop of protein powder
  • 1 cup of milk or a plant-based alternative
  • Optional: a handful of spinach or a few berries for added nutrients

Tips for Keeping Costs Low

  • Buy in Bulk: Purchase staples like oats, rice, and lentils in bulk to save money.
  • Seasonal Produce: Opt for seasonal fruits and vegetables which are often cheaper and fresher.
  • Frozen Vegetables: These can be more affordable and have a longer shelf life while retaining their nutritional value.
  • Meal Prep: Preparing meals in advance can help avoid the temptation of expensive takeout and ensure you stick to your diet plan.
